There are a lot of talented musicians with a little bit of spare time and the desire to help others learn. There are even more beginning music students who could benefit from private lessons. This is a centralized online marketplace that will allow students to find teachers that are nearby and meet their needs. Rate your instructors and give recommendations.

Teachers benefit from being able to advertise their services to a wider variety of students without giving a percentage to the music stores. Students can find information about their teachers including specialities, ratings from other students, and rates while also saving money by not paying the music store overhead.

FAQ

How do I find a teacher?
Use our search form to enter information with your instrument and years of experience and we will show a list of teachers in your area that match your needs. You will be able to view their rates, ratings, and philosophy of teaching to understand if they would be a good match.
What does Improve Your Tune cost to use?
Students can join and search for free. Teachers pay a fee to join and small monthly fee to participate in the marketplace.
