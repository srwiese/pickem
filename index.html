FAQ

How do I find a teacher?
Use our search form to enter information with your instrument and years of experience and we will show a list of teachers in your area that match your needs. You will be able to view their rates, ratings, and philosophy of teaching to understand if they would be a good match.
What does Improve Your Tune cost to use?
Students can join and search for free. Teachers pay a fee to join and small monthly fee to participate in the marketplace.
